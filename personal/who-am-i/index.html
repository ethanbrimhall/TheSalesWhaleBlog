Blog Post #1 - Who Am I?

Published on February 19, 2019

Welcome to the Blog

Yep! I know what you’re thinking here’s another sales blogger! Bingo! Yes, I am a salesman and this is my first blog. When I hit the send button it will be official. I will receive the covenant title: a Blogger.

First, as always thank you for coming to my 1st blog and I value your time.

I will share with you who I am, who my support system team is, and why I am doing this.

Family

First, my name is TL......yep that is right, TL. I was given that by a mentor. I’ll save that story for another day. My support team consists of my beautiful wife Tracy, my sharp/talented son Ethan (my IT guy), and last but not least my strong brave warrior Madison.










My Experiences

I have over 20 years of selling products/services as well as leading, coaching, and growing professional sales teams. My clients include small business to large Fortune 500 customers. I have also spent many many seasons in the corporate sector as well.

Why Am I Doing This?

Simple, there are so many blogs out there with high reaching, overly complicated sales techniques that lack simplicity. Many of these ideals are taught by individuals who never worked in the sales trenches. I say “knowledge with no experience is worthless.”

What I want to share with you are simple, clean, and effective sales techniques that work for today’s customers, clients, and members. I will blog greatly about

  • relationship selling, because the first sale to be made is you the salesperson.
  • proven effective skills that gave myself and my teams a winning edge.
  • how to evolve as a salesperson to keep pace with our everchanging markets
  • how to navigate through a toxic work environment or toxic leader
  • sales barriers and objection preventions...not overcoming but preventing.

Aim high today and always give your best! - TL
