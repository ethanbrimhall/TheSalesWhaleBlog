Selling 101 – Best Ways to Start the Sale Off Right!

Published on February 21, 2019

As we know, a sale can be killed in the first 10 mins from an improper greeting. Here are several SIMPLE key points on making the right first impression.

1. Arrive Early

Do not be late. Arrive a few mins early. If you are on time, you are late.


2. Be prepared and professional

You are being watched as you exit your vehicle – respect their property. Where do you park? Their grass is beautiful, don’t walk in it.


3. Extend a warm greeting

A smile is the best way to be likeable. Step back a few feet and give them space as you are invited in.


4. Now it’s time for the warm-up.

Be genuine here. Don’t sound like a gimmicky salesperson. Find the customer's passion where you can insert a compliment. Keep it about them. Customers like to be complimented. Enjoy a brief mood breaking conversation with your customer to lighten the atmosphere. Customers buy from people they like.


5. Transition

Find a place in the home to sit down with your clients and talk about the agenda along with the next steps.

We can spend hours on role-playing presentations, opening techniques, price conditioning, and closing skills. But if we don’t nail this most important and yet simple part, it can break the sale.

Be your self and be authentic. Your customers will appreciate that.

Aim high today and always give your best! - TL
